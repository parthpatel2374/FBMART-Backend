Shipping & Return Policy

Shipping Policy

Shipping Costs: Free standard shipping on orders over ₹3,500. A flat rate of ₹200 applies to orders below ₹3,500. Expedited shipping options are available at checkout.

Delivery Timeframes:

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days.
  • Expedited Shipping: 2-3 business days.
  • Same-Day/Next-Day Shipping: Available for orders placed by 2 PM IST, subject to location availability.

Order Processing: Orders are processed within 1-2 business days, excluding weekends and public holidays.

Order Tracking: Tracking details will be emailed once your order ships.

Shipping Restrictions: We currently ship only within India. PO Boxes or restricted areas are excluded from delivery.

Return Policy

Eligibility for Returns: Returns are accepted within 14 days of receiving the product. Items must be unused, in their original condition, with tags and packaging intact.

Non-Returnable Items:

  • Personalized or custom-made items.
  • Perishable goods such as food or flowers.
  • Final sale or clearance items.

Process for Returns: Contact our support team via email or phone to initiate a return. Provide proof of purchase, and a return shipping label will be issued (if applicable).

Refunds: Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 7 business days of receiving the returned item. Note: Shipping fees are non-refundable unless the item is defective.

Exchanges: Defective or damaged items can be exchanged. Contact customer service for assistance.

Damaged or Defective Items: Report damaged or defective items within 48 hours of delivery. Provide photographic evidence for verification.
